About Us

Nisrag Seva Samiti is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainable development. We believe in the power of trees to transform communities and combat climate change.

Our mission is to plant trees, create awareness about environmental protection, and work with rural communities to build a greener, healthier India.

Our History

2020

Nisrag Seva Samiti was founded with a vision to bring green change to rural India.

2021

Planted our first 1,000 trees in local villages. Started community awareness programs.

2022

Expanded to 5 new districts. Reached 10,000 trees milestone.

2023

Partnered with government agencies. Won recognition for environmental work.

2024

Planted over 50,000 trees. Created green corridors in rural areas.

2025

Launched youth internship program. Now with 500+ active volunteers.
